BMI Calculator

GetFit - BMI calculator and fitness tracker

Source code

Year: 2023

Technologies included: Java language, Android studio.

Abstract

GetFit is a piece of software that, with just one click, eliminates the need to manually calculate and find someone's BMI. This app accepts input in multiple measurement systems that may not be provided by other apps.The basic goal is to keep people healthy. The BMI App provides us with information on our current and past BMI. We receive all the information, including whether we are overweight or underweight, when we enter our height and weight.


Motivation of the app

GetFit is a BMI calculator and fitness tracker designed to monitor progress and offer motivation for health conscious users. GetFit allows users to easily calculate their BMI from both metric and imperial unit inputs and stores their results for later reference. The intent is that users will be able to look back on their progress and be motivated to continue their exercise regime.


Feature specifications

  1. BMI calculator in imperial and metric units
  2. Automatic tracking of previous calculations including input parameters and date
  3. Personalized user greeting
  4. List of useful links for further reading
