Networking Security

The Next-Gen Network Topology

Project Link

Year: 2023

Technologies included: VM, PF-sense, Window server/LDAP, email server.

Abstract

Since cyberattacks are becoming commonplace in our daily lives, our team has chosen to design a network topology to address these problems. Numerous servers in the network topology will be accessible to the general public, along with a few private servers. Our goal is to deploy firewalls like pf-sense to meet our security requirements and offer secure network traffic because security plays a significant role in the topology of our network. In order to authenticate users, we will also set up a Windows server using LDAP. For our public server, we will build up a nextcloud server so that approved personnel may safely share files and photographs. In order to receive notice of potential dangers, we will also set up an email server.


Our main goal is to provide a secure network topology to prevent cyber-attacks and provide an overall better topology for businesses, and people alike. With the proper implementation of our project, we would successfully deliver a more secure and streamlined network.

Project Outcomes

The purpose of this research is to show how a safe network topology can help prevent a cyberattack. Enterprises are less likely to experience data loss and theft by using an effective network security solution. Thanks to cybersecurity, dangerous spyware is kept off of your computer. Furthermore, it ensures the safety of shared data. Network security boosts client and consumer confidence and protects your business from the damaging legal and reputational repercussions of a security breach.


Protection from outside threats. Although most cyberattacks are started for financial gain, their goals can be as diverse as their perpetrators. These negative actors, including industrial spies, hacktivists, and cybercriminals, all have one thing in common: how swiftly, deftly, and covertly they carry out their attacks.
